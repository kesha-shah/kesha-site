About

Hi, I'm Kesha. I've been working in tech since I graduated from university. After about a decade, I started feeling that it wasn't what I wanted anymore. I still love to build, to solve problems, and to automate things, but I want to work more on my inner peace and happiness than just toward making more money.

To experience that, I left my job and started building a slower, simpler life. These days I'm focusing more on my health, fitness, yoga, and reading that guides me toward a more spiritual path. I'm also enjoying cooking and trying new recipes, which feels creative and mindful to me.

And I still build. I've been playing with AI to build faster and bring ideas to life. Small, useful tools that solve real problems in my own life, things like running our farm, managing family finances, timing my interval workouts, filling tedious paperwork, and plenty more, which I share openly in case they help someone else too.

  • Mutual Funds Analytics Dashboard

    Live

    I built this because I wanted my financial data to stay mine, and the specific analytics that third-party apps just don't offer. It pulls my CAMS statement from Gmail and shows holdings, XIRR, allocation and LTCG tax, all locally and fully encrypted. I can track my whole family's portfolio in one place, and nothing ever leaves the machine.
